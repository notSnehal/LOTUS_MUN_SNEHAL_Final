ABOUT LOTUS MUN

LOTUSMUN is a global platform for beginning and intermediate Model United Nations enthusiasts to hone their skills of diplomacy, public speaking, and critical thinking.It was first hosted in 2011, LotusMun'23 has been a pioneer in the MUN circuit, both online and offline, and has paved the way and set an example for conferences.With immense pride, we announce that one of the most prestigious MUNs in Delhi NCR is now back as LotusMun'23: Ambassadors of Hope. Besides being an excellent opportunity to engage in meaningful discussions and forge new connections, the numerous cultural activities that LOTUSMUN23 includes will make the event even more unforgettable.
